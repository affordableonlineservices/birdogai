# BirDogAi Sales Scripts

Two scripts in this document:

1. **60-second walkthrough** — for live screen-shares with prospects (Zoom, in-person, etc.). Memorize the beats, deliver naturally.
2. **2-minute Loom demo flow** — click-by-click instructions to record a polished demo video that hits all 7 capabilities.

---

# SCRIPT 1 — 60-Second Walkthrough (Live Screen Share)

**Use this when:** A prospect on a sales call asks "show me what it does."

**Setup before sharing screen:**
- Demo open in browser, full screen
- "Deal Sourcing" tab selected
- Phone on silent
- Have your contact card visible somewhere

**The script:**

---

> **[0:00 – 0:08] Open with the value prop**
>
> "So this is BirDogAi. The way I think about it: it's the bird dog you'd hire if you could clone yourself five times. It hunts deals, runs the numbers, drafts the outreach, and reviews the contracts — all the grunt work you don't want to do."

---

> **[0:08 – 0:20] Show the dashboard**
>
> *(Point to the top stats bar.)*
>
> "First thing you see when you log in: 2,847 properties scanned overnight. Seven actually pencil. Three are hot. About 38 hours saved this week alone."
>
> *(Point to the markets pills.)*
>
> "I'm hunting Tampa and Birmingham right now, but you can run any market."

---

> **[0:20 – 0:35] Show one deal in detail**
>
> *(Click the first deal card — 4218 W Bay Ave.)*
>
> "Here's a deal it found this morning. Probate sale, motivated seller. ARV is 310, asking 185, my profit after a 42K rehab is around 52K. And see this gold box? That's BirDog telling me *why* this deal — comps support the ARV, neighborhood is right, here's the offer it'd recommend."

---

> **[0:35 – 0:48] Show the underwriting tab**
>
> *(Click "Underwriting" tab. Drag the ARV slider.)*
>
> "Wherever there's a number, I can stress-test it. ARV drops 5%? Watch — still pencils. Drops 10%? Now it's risky and BirDog tells me to walk. I run this on every deal in 30 seconds instead of 30 minutes."

---

> **[0:48 – 0:60] Close with the chat**
>
> *(Scroll up to the inline chatbot. Click the "Find wholesale deals in Tampa" prompt.)*
>
> "And if I just want to ask — like a real assistant — it answers. Three wholesale deals, all under 150K, ready to make offers on."
>
> *(Pause. Look at prospect.)*
>
> "That's the whole thing. What questions can I answer?"

---

**Key delivery tips:**

- Speak slower than feels natural — prospects need time to absorb
- After each section, pause for 1 second to let it land
- If they interrupt with a question, *answer it* — don't fight to finish the script
- The chatbot demo is your closer; don't skip it even if you're running short
- End with "what questions" — never "does that make sense?" (yes/no closes the conversation)

---

**If you have an extra 30 seconds:**

Show the **Document Review** tab. Click the purchase agreement. Say:
> "And on the back end — every contract you sign goes through BirDog first. It flags risky clauses, opportunities, and pulls every term that matters. This contract had a 7-day inspection window when standard is 14 — caught and flagged in 4 seconds."

# SCRIPT 2 — 2-Minute Loom Demo Flow (Recorded Video)

**Use this when:** Sending a recorded demo to leads who didn't book a live call, or embedding on the landing page.

**Setup before recording:**
- Browser at full screen
- Loom set to "Cam + Screen" — your face in the corner
- Demo open at the dashboard, "Deal Sourcing" tab active
- All other tabs closed
- Notifications silenced
- Mic check — say one sentence at full volume to confirm

**The flow:**

---

## [0:00 – 0:10] HOOK

**On screen:** Dashboard, top stats bar visible
**Mouse:** Static, hovering over BirDogAi logo
**Say:**
> "If you're a real estate investor or wholesaler, you spend most of your time on grunt work — pulling lists, running comps, drafting letters, reviewing contracts. I built BirDogAi to do all of that for you. Let me show you in two minutes."

---

## [0:10 – 0:25] CAPABILITY 1 — Deal Sourcing

**Mouse:** Hover over the dashboard stats, then scroll down to the deal list
**Click:** First deal card (4218 W Bay Ave) to highlight it
**Say:**
> "First — deal sourcing. BirDog scanned 2,847 properties overnight. Seven pencil, three are hot. Each deal shows the score, the strategy, the profit, and *why* BirDog flagged it. Probate sale, motivated seller, comps support the ARV. I didn't lift a finger for any of this."

---

## [0:25 – 0:40] CAPABILITY 2 — Underwriting

**Click:** "Underwriting" tab in sidebar
**Action:** Drag the ARV slider down 10%, then back. Click "Run Stress Test."
**Say:**
> "Second — underwriting. Drop in any property and BirDog runs the full pro forma. Watch — I can stress-test in real time. ARV drops 5%, deal still pencils. Drops 10%, BirDog tells me to walk. Stress test gives me five scenarios in one click."

---

## [0:40 – 0:55] CAPABILITY 3 — Document Review

**Click:** "Document Review" tab
**Action:** Make sure Purchase Agreement is highlighted; let the flags appear on screen
**Say:**
> "Third — every contract goes through BirDog before I sign. It flagged that this purchase agreement only gives me a 7-day inspection window — standard is 14. It also caught a $4,500 seller credit buried in the addendum that improves my margin. Every term that matters, extracted in seconds."

---

## [0:55 – 1:10] CAPABILITY 4 — Market Research

**Click:** "Market Research" tab
**Action:** Let the Tampa snapshot render
**Say:**
> "Fourth — markets. One-page brief on any zip in 30 seconds. Population growth, jobs, rent trends, what's working, what to watch. This used to take me a week of research; now it takes me a coffee."

---

## [1:10 – 1:25] CAPABILITY 5 — Tenant Operations

**Click:** "Tenant Ops" tab
**Action:** Click "Late rent reminder" button
**Say:**
> "Fifth — tenant operations. Late rent reminder, maintenance response, lease renewals, screening — all drafted in BirDog's voice, in your tone. This message landed in seconds; I review and send."

---

## [1:25 – 1:40] CAPABILITIES 6 & 7 — IR + Outreach (rapid)

**Click:** "Investor Relations" tab → pause for 2 seconds → **Click:** "Seller Outreach" tab
**Say:**
> "If you syndicate, BirDog drafts your monthly investor updates from raw data. If you do outreach, it writes personalized cold seller letters at scale — last campaign hit a 9.2% reply rate."

---

## [1:40 – 1:55] LIVE CHAT — the closer

**Action:** Scroll to the top, to the inline "Try BirDog Live" chatbot
**Click:** "🎯 Find wholesale deals in Tampa" suggested prompt
**Say:**
> "And if I just want to ask — like a real assistant — it answers. Wholesale deals in Tampa under 150K, ready to make offers on, in three seconds."

---

## [1:55 – 2:00] CALL TO ACTION

**Action:** Hover over the gold contact bar at the top OR the "Book a Free Demo" button in the footer
**Say:**
> "If you want BirDog hunting in your market, email me at affordablepropertiestoday@gmail.com or call 702-337-3494. I'll personally analyze one of your deals — free — within 24 hours. Talk soon."

# Recording Tips for the Loom

**Before you record:**

- Run through it once without recording. Fix any flow issues.
- Check audio levels — speak as if the listener is across a coffee table, not a stadium.
- Record in **1080p** if Loom Pro, otherwise 720p.
- If you stumble more than twice in a section, restart that section — Loom lets you trim.
- Look at your **camera lens** when delivering the hook and the CTA. Looking at the screen the rest of the time is fine.

**After you record:**

- **Trim** the dead air at the start and end.
- **Title** it: "BirDogAi — 2-minute demo for real estate investors"
- **Custom thumbnail:** A clear screenshot of the dashboard with the gold deal-score badges visible. Loom auto-generates one but a custom one converts better.
- **CTA in description:** "Get your own free deal analysis: affordablepropertiestoday@gmail.com · (702) 337-3494"
- **Embed it:** On the Carrd landing page (Section 6 spot is good), in your email signature, in cold outreach replies.
