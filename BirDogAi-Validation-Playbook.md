# BirDogAi — Validation Playbook

Your goal at this stage is NOT to build a marketing machine. It's to find out, in the next 2-3 weeks, whether real Arizona investors will pay for what you've built. Everything below is designed to get you 20 conversations and 3-5 paying founding members at zero or near-zero cost.

---

## The 60-second pitch (verbal — for meetups and live conversations)

Memorize the beats, deliver naturally. Don't recite it.

> "Quick question — how much time do you spend each week pulling comps, running pro formas, and writing seller letters? Yeah, exactly. So I built something for that.
>
> It's called BirDogAi. Think of it as a bird dog you'd hire if you could clone yourself. You give it your buy-box — markets, numbers, strategy — and overnight it scans Zillow, MLS, county records, off-market lists, and surfaces only deals that pencil. Full underwriting in 30 seconds: ARV, repairs, cash flow, IRR, stress tests, recommended offer, all explained in plain English.
>
> It also reads contracts and flags risky clauses, drafts your seller outreach, handles tenant stuff if you're a landlord — basically the seven things every investor hates doing.
>
> I'm doing free deal analysis for Arizona investors right now in exchange for honest feedback. Want me to run one of your deals tonight?"

**Delivery notes:**

- Open with a question — gets them talking, not listening passively.
- Pause after "so I built something for that." Let them ask "what is it?"
- The "free deal analysis in exchange for feedback" is your Trojan horse. Don't say "buy" or "$97/month" in the first conversation.
- End with a specific ask. "Want me to run one of your deals tonight?" beats "let me know if you're interested."

## The 60-second pitch (DM / LinkedIn / forum post version)

Three lines max. Anything longer gets ignored.

> Hey {{name}} — saw you invest in {{Phoenix/Tucson/wherever}}. I built an AI tool that pulls comps, runs full underwriting (ARV, repairs, cash flow, IRR), and drafts seller outreach in 30 seconds. Built it because I got tired of scrolling Zillow at 2am.
>
> I'm running free analyses on real deals for AZ investors this month in exchange for honest feedback. Got a property you're sizing up? Send me the address and you'll have a full report tomorrow.
>
> Either way, good hunting.

That's it. No links. No deck. No "happy to hop on a call." Just an offer.

## The 2-week validation plan

**Week 1 — Get to 10 conversations**

- Day 1: Post in BiggerPockets Phoenix forum, Tucson FB group, Reddit. (1 hour)
- Day 2-3: Send 30 LinkedIn DMs to AZ investors. (2 hours total)
- Day 4: Attend AZREIA Phoenix or the next available meetup. Demo to 5 people. (1 evening)
- Day 5-7: Run free deal analyses for everyone who said yes. Schedule 15-min feedback calls.

**Week 2 — Convert and learn**

- Run the analyses, then on each feedback call ask three questions:
  1. "On a scale of 1-10, how useful was this?"
  2. "Would you pay $97/month to have this running on every deal you look at?"
  3. "What would you need to see to say yes today?"
- Anyone who answers 8+ on usefulness AND yes on payment → offer founding member spot ($97/mo, locked rate, immediate onboarding)
- Anyone who answers 6-7 → ask what would make it a 9. That's your roadmap.
- Anyone who answers 1-5 → ask why. That's your pivot signal.

**The number that matters:** if 3 out of 20 conversations end with "yes, I'll pay today" — you have a real business and you can confidently spend money on the cold email machine. If 0 out of 20 do, don't blame the marketing — go back to the product, the offer, or the audience.

## What to bring to a meetup

- Laptop with the demo open and warmed up (live data loaded, not a static screenshot)
- Phone charged for the followup contact-exchange
- 20 simple business cards — name, BirDogAi, phone, website, "free deal analysis for AZ investors" tagline
- A 1-page tearsheet (optional) — what BirDogAi does in bullet form, your phone, your website. Hand it to anyone who's interested but doesn't have time to demo right then.

Don't bring a slide deck. Don't bring a 20-minute walkthrough. Open laptop, show the deal sourcing tab, show one deal end-to-end, ask if they want to send you a property tonight.

## Quick scripts for specific situations

**They say "send me more info":**
> "Happy to — give me your phone, I'll text you the link to my demo right now. What's a property address you're considering? I'll have a full analysis to you tomorrow."

(Always trade your "send me info" for their "send me a deal." Keeps the conversation alive.)

**They say "how much is it?":**
> "$97/month for founding members, locked rate forever. But the analysis I'm offering you is free — let me prove it works on your deals first, then we can talk about that."

**They say "I already use PropStream / DealMachine / [X]":**
> "Cool — those give you data. BirDog gives you decisions. PropStream tells you a house exists; BirDog tells you whether to buy it, what to offer, and writes the letter. Want to see the difference on a real deal?"

**They say "I'm not really interested":**
> "Totally fair. If you ever want a free underwriting on a deal, my offer stands — no strings. Good hunting out there." *(Move on. Don't push. Reputation matters in a small market.)*

## What to track

A simple Google Sheet or Notion table — five columns:

| Date | Name | Source (meetup/DM/post) | Demo'd? | Free analysis sent? | Feedback score (1-10) | Paid? |

After 20 rows you'll know if you have a business. After 50 you'll know exactly what to build next.

## Final note

You can run this entire validation phase for the cost of a meetup ticket and gas. No domains, no Smartlead subscription, no list-building tools, no $200/month sending stack. Save that money for after you've heard "yes, I'll pay" out of 3-5 real investors' mouths.

Then build the email machine. With proof. With case studies. With a script you know works because real people used it to close real deals.

That's the order: validate, then scale.
