# BirDogAi — Carrd Landing Page Build Guide

This is your complete copy-and-paste pack for building the BirDogAi landing page in Carrd. Designed for the **Carrd Pro Standard plan** ($19/yr), which gives you forms, custom domain, and analytics. You can also build this on the free plan if you skip the form widget and use a mailto button instead.

---

## STEP 1 — Carrd setup (5 minutes)

1. Sign up at **carrd.co** → click **Build a site**
2. Pick the template **"Profile" (one of the simple single-section templates)** — we'll heavily edit it
3. Site name: `birdogai` (becomes birdogai.carrd.co — claim a custom domain later)
4. **Settings → Style:**
   - Background color: `#fdfcf8` (cream)
   - Accent color: `#c9a961` (gold)
   - Heading font: **Playfair Display** (Google Fonts → add it in Carrd's font settings)
   - Body font: **Inter**
5. **Settings → Form** (only on Pro plan): connect to your email or Mailchimp/ConvertKit/Google Sheets

### SECTION 4 — What BirDog does (7 capability cards)

Use Carrd's **Gallery** or **Columns** widget. Three rows of cards (3 + 3 + 1 on desktop, single column on mobile).

For each card: small icon (use Carrd's icon library), title, one-line description.

**1. Deal Sourcing** 🎯
Monitors Zillow, Redfin, MLS, county records, off-market lists overnight — surfaces only deals that match your buy-box.

**2. Underwriting** 📊
Full pro forma in 30 seconds. ARV, repairs, cash flow, IRR, stress tests — explained in plain English.

**3. Document Review** 📄
Reads contracts, leases, title reports. Flags red clauses. Extracts every term that matters.

**4. Market Research** 🏙️
Population, jobs, rents, migration, zoning. One-page market briefs on any zip in 30 seconds.

**5. Tenant Operations** 🔧
Drafts late-rent reminders, screens applicants, schedules maintenance, handles renewals.

**6. Investor Relations** 💼
For syndicators: monthly updates, distribution waterfalls, capital call Q&A, personalized LP outreach.

**7. Seller Outreach** ✉️
Cold letters, follow-up sequences, negotiation drafts — personalized at scale, last campaign hit 9.2% reply rate.

## STEP 4 — Launch checklist

Before you share the URL with anyone:

- [ ] Submit a test form yourself — confirm the email arrives
- [ ] Click every button — nothing should be a dead link
- [ ] Test the mailto and tel links on a phone
- [ ] Read every word out loud — catch typos and awkward phrasing
- [ ] Show it to one trusted investor friend before pushing to your audience
- [ ] Set up a **simple thank-you page** in Carrd (form redirect → "Got your request, BirDog is sniffing now. Reply within 24 hrs.")
- [ ] Add a Calendly booking link as the secondary CTA on the thank-you page

## Bonus — what to A/B test once it's live

1. **Headline:** "Your AI bird dog never sleeps" vs. "Find better deals while you sleep" vs. "Stop scrolling Zillow"
2. **Lead magnet:** Free deal analysis vs. Free market brief vs. 7-day trial
3. **Pricing display:** Single founding-member offer vs. 3-tier comparison
4. **Hero button text:** "Get a free deal analysis" vs. "Try it on my deal" vs. "Show me what BirDog finds"
5. **Social proof position:** Above pricing vs. below pricing

Run each test for 2 weeks minimum or until you have 200+ visitors per variant.
